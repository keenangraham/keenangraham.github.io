Hi, I'm Keenan Graham!

I spend most of my time writing code for The ENCODE Project. Previously I looked under a microscope for intestinal parasites and worked on a project to determine the prevalence of Blastocystis hominis and other intestinal protozoa in over 120,000 clinical samples. The code used for wrangling and visualizing the project data can be found on my GitHub.
